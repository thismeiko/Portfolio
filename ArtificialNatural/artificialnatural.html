ARTIFICIAL? NATURAL?

Year : 2022

media : Interaction

Natural environment, artificial environment, what is the difference between the effects of these environments on plants? I just look at the plants and it's hard to tell what environment they're in.

The natural environment is being invaded by conventional waste, electronic waste. This change has quietly affected the development of other life as well. Does nature really need humans? While the environment is being destroyed with all kinds of garbage, we are artificially cultivating the "natural" environment for plants to live, thrive and develop "natural" results.
