ETERNAL MEMORY

Year : 2020

media : Web design

In recent years, the topic of fashion and environmental protection has attracted much attention. Many environmentalists and animal protection enthusiasts have resisted the extravagance, waste and killing of animals in the fashion industry through demonstrations. At the same time, the fashion industry has gradually noticed this problem and tried to reduce pollution and waste in its own way. This is my inspiration for creating this website. The purpose of this website is to encourage people to buy second-hand goods to reduce pollution, rebuild and use waste and old things; at the same time, it also provides vintage clothing fans a platform to exchange and share stories between them and their clothes.
